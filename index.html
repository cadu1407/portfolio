About Me

Hello! My name is Carlos Eduardo Cardoso Barreiros. I am 17 years old and currently enrolled in the second semester of Computer Science at Anhembi Morumbi University. I completed my primary education in 2022 at Adventist College of Liberdade. I have always had a passion for computers and a natural aptitude for related subjects. Initially, I aspired to become a civil engineer due to my strong mathematical skills, but that field experienced a decline in the market. It was then that my father recommended this field to me, and in early 2022, I began delving into it, taking relevant courses and developing a strong affinity for the field. Presently, I have knowledge in Java, MySQL, UML, System Requirements, and some proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JS. I am also actively seeking to enhance my fluency in the English language and currently enrolled in an English course at CNA language school, where I am at an Advanced Level 1. Some of my skills include being focused, organized, a team player, having good interpersonal relationships, and possessing a strong aptitude for learning.
